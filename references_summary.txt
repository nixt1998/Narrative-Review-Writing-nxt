NOTE ON GRAY LITERATURE
================================================================================

Reference [10] (Paez 2017, J Evid Based Med) discusses gray literature search
methodologies. While this reference informed our understanding of comprehensive
literature searching, the narrative-review skill's protocol operates exclusively
on data from verified, peer-reviewed published literature. No content from gray
literature sources (preprints, conference abstracts, theses, government reports,
etc.) is incorporated into the manuscript unless the user explicitly provides
and verifies such sources through the standard PDF verification pipeline.
